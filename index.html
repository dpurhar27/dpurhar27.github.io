Evolution of Finance

In the future the worlds value will be represented as digital assets, also known as cryptographic tokens.

Acentian will be the ultimate risk-management solution for digital assets.

Scenario: Susan owns a non-fungible digital asset that represents her house and property. The local school is being shut-down. Susan fears her property will lose value. She is not going to sell her property and move. Instead she goes on Acentian's global market and short sells her property. Her property does lose value and she closes her short sell. Instead of losing her money Susan has successfully hedged her potential loss.

This is possible because of the transparency, accessability, security and liquidity global markets will have on a blockchain with tokenized assets.

What is Acentian solving?

Current financial markets have several flaws that have been considered acceptable. With the latest blockchain and digital asset technology these flaws no longer need to exist.
Risk Exposure
  • Problem: There are limited market instruments that expose people to risk management for digital assets.

  • Fix: We offer financial derivative contracts that support on-chain and off-chain assets giving people the ability to manage their risk.

  • Stablecoins allow us to create traditional pairs on-chain and expose our users to risk management in their local currency such as USD.

  • This allows us to apply our platforms benefits to traditional markets and expand our target market to the biggest financial market in the world.
Market Features
  • Problem: Existing markets have several major flaws including no market transparency, low security, high barriers for accessability and limited functionality.

  • Fix: We solve all of these flaws by building our market with breaking blockchain and cryptocurrency technology. Using the Ethereum blockchain enables the native use of smart contracts, ERC-20 and ERC-721 tokens.

  • With our hybrid approach, our market is able to support superior features while offering better and more functionality to end-users.
User Experience
  • Problem: Applications using the blockchain suffer from scaling issues and latency leading to a terrible user experience.

  • Fix: To create a real-time efficient market we're using Sidechains & Plasma, a new scaling solution for blockchains and decentralized applications.

  • Sidechains & Plasma enable a greater user experience through instant transactions, no transaction fees and a smoother process while keeping the utility of blockchains.

  • This nullifies the one quality traditional markets had over our platforms market.
